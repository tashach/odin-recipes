Easy Wonton Soup

A great weeknight meal, this recipe takes advantage of frozen wontons and home made chicken broth to provide a comforting, quick dinner.

Ingredients

  • Frozen wontons, preferably pork and shrimp.
  • 4 heads baby bok choy, whites and greens separated
  • 4 cups home made chicken broth
  • Sliced scallions, white pepper for serving

Cooking Steps

  1. Cook frozen wontons according to package directions.
  2. Bring chicken broth to simmer. Add bok choy whites and cook until tender.
  3. Add wonton greens.
  4. Ladle desired amount of wontons into bowl, and cover with broth and greens.
  5. Garnish with white pepper and scallions if desired. Serve.
