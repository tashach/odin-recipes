Simple Eggs and Rice

A quick meal made with pantry ingredients, so simple it almost doesn't need a recipe. Makes a great, protein packed breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • 2 eggs
  • Golden Mountain Seasoning Sauce
  • Kimchi, for serving

Cooking Steps

  1. Heat 1tbsp vegetable oil in a medium nonstick pain over high heat until lightly smoking.
  2. Crack 2 eggs directly into pan, turn heat down to medium.
  3. When whites are nearly cooked through, carefully flip the eggs, trying not to break the yolk.
  4. Cook until whites are just set. Season with Golden Mountain.
  5. Serve eggs over hot rice with kimchi on the side.
